Why WhistleDrop?

WhistleDrop provides a secure channel for whistleblowers to share important information with journalists while maintaining complete anonymity. Our platform uses state-of-the-art encryption and operates as a Tor Hidden Service to protect your identity.

How We Protect Your Security

End-to-End Encryption

Your files are encrypted immediately upon upload and can only be decrypted by authorized journalists.

Tor Hidden Service

WhistleDrop operates as a Tor Hidden Service, providing network-level anonymity and protection.

No Metadata Storage

We don't store IP addresses, browser information, or other metadata that could identify you.

How to Use WhistleDrop Securely

  1. Use the Tor Browser - Download and use the Tor Browser to access WhistleDrop.
  2. Remove Metadata - Before uploading documents, remove identifying metadata from files.
  3. Use Secure Devices - Use a secure device that isn't linked to your identity.
  4. Clear Your Traces - After using WhistleDrop, clear your browser cache and close all windows.
